This data looks at the spending made by Super PAC's on behalf of political candidates. The size and height of a circle represents how much was spent on a specific candidate at a given point in time. You can move your mouse over each circle to see the date, candidate, and super pac of the expenditure. See if you can tell who is spending the most!

There are two data sets. The first one is a sample data set being served from our servers at vizuly.io. The second one is live data from The Sunlight Foundation.. Sometimes the live data does not return a full data set and you will see less circles.
